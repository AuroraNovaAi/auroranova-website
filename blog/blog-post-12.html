How Has AI Changed Web Design?

Published: February 10, 2025 | Category: Artificial Intelligence

AI tools have dramatically accelerated design workflows, but the gap between AI-generated and human-crafted design is still very real — and still very visible.

In the past two years, AI design tools have accelerated many stages of the process: from concept generation and wireframing to color palette suggestions and copywriting. Figma's AI features, visual generation via Midjourney, and content drafting with ChatGPT have all become part of professional workflows. Tasks that once took hours can now be completed in minutes. But speed is not always synonymous with quality. AI learns and reproduces statistical averages — it reflects a kind of mean of existing designs. Truly original work, deeply embedded in a brand's DNA and resonating with its specific audience, still requires human creativity, market understanding, and intuitive decision-making. At AuroraNova we treat AI as a working partner: to accelerate repetitive tasks, generate alternative ideas, and compress the prototyping phase. But the final call — what is right for this client and why — always belongs to our human team. AI saves us time; we use that time to think better.
